Commonly Asked Questions

1) How do I log in or sign up? Answer: Click the Log in or Sign up button on the right side of the Nav bar located at the top of page.

2) How can I search for a Park? Answer: You can search by state for a park by click the search button in the nav bar. In the search area you will see some drop downs that you can choose from.

3) How can I add a park to my favorite's list? Answer: When you have searched for your parks you will see a star next to the park name click the star and that will add it to your favorite's list.
