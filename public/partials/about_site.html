ParketList

Welcome

* For those that want to see the wonderful possiblities of adventure and discover what might be right under their nose, Parketlist is the place for you!

* Start out by either making an account or logging in.

* Once you have that out of the way you can search for the National Parks that are in your state or other states.

* You are then able to save, delete or update your hopeful destinations.

* As an add feature you can sign up for the pro package which will allow you to add notes to your favorite parks.
